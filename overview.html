Problem Statement

Week 3-4 Assigment

Hiring a lawyer can get pretty expensive in Pakistan. Two non-technical investors thought of an idea to create an AI-based lawyer that can process textual queries and answer them accordingly with citations to the PPC (Pakistan Penal Code) and Constitution. This is especially useful for people living in remote areas and even urban women looking to get a consultation privately. Luckily, you run a software development business and will be providing them services.

Discover and Develop

  1. For this week, you will explore either Materialize CSS to assist in styling your website. You will not be using a video to learn this library, instead you will use the documentation.

  2. Secondly, you will explore dribbble. Dribbble is a website that primarily caters to designers and helps them in building their portfolio. However, as a frontend developer, you will use this to find inspiration for your websites. Try writing “landing page” in the search bar to get an idea.
